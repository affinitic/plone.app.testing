Plone testing helpers
--------------------

This package contains various test helpers. They are all importable from
``plone.app.testing`` directly, or from their canonical locations at
``plone.app.testing.helpers``.

    >>> from plone.app.testing import helpers

For testing, we need a testrunner

    >>> from zope.testing.testrunner import runner
